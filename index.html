Matrix Strategy

Procurement (River)

Essential for raw material access (Carp/Buffalo). Usually lacks big freezing.

Processors (Coastal)

High freezing/packing capacity. Usually distant from river supply.

Cold Storage Hubs

Pure infrastructure plays for inventory buffering.

The Winning Move:

Acquire a Top-Left node for supply, and truck product to a Bottom-Right or Top-Right facility for freezing.
